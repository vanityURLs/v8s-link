Report harmful or unsafe use

If a short link on this instance appears to be used for phishing, malware, spam, impersonation, harassment, security concerns, or another harmful purpose, report it to the instance operator.

What to include

Include the short URL, the destination you reached, the reason it appears abusive, and any relevant screenshots or timestamps. Do not include sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for the report.

Response

The operator may disable unsafe links, update block policies, or take other action needed to protect visitors and the reputation of the short domain.

Other concerns

For urgent security incidents, legal concerns, or anything that does not fit another policy page, use the operator's preferred private contact if one is published.
